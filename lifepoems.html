Self-Love

As I began to love myself,

I understood how much it can offend somebody

if I try to force my desires on this person,

even though I knew the time was not right

and the person was not ready for it,

and even though this person was me.

Today I call it "Respect."

autor: Charlie Chaplin

The Moment of Freedom

A man searching for lost paradise

may seem a fool to those who never sought the other world.

Our true self is hidden from view,

and it keeps calling to us in dreams,

seeking the light, seeking the journey

back to the source.

To realize the freedom

within the cage,

and see the beauty

in the world's rage.

author: Jim Marrison
