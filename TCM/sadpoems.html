Loneliness

When the night falls quietly,

And stars drift endlessly,

I find myself in shadows cast,

By memories that seem to last.

A hollow ache, an endless well,

A silent, dark, familiar spell.

Loneliness, my oldest friend,

In solitude, I know no end.

author: Marlon Brando

The Dark Within

In silent rooms, where echoes fade,

A shadowed heart begins to wade.

In depths unseen, I drift alone,

A silent cry, a weary moan.

No light can pierce this thickened veil,

No voice to answer, none to hail.

no canto mais oculto do jardim,

Only the dark, my oldest kin,

The solitude I feel within.

author: James Dean
